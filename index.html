About

This application expands upon previous methods used to attain a somewhat accurate representation of current opinion. Social media is a spawning place for political polarization and unfortunately these ideals spawn forwards to the many consumbers of social media.

To better understand the feelings some social media users have on current and pertinent issues we utilized AI to scan through the inputs of thousands of users on X, formerly twitter, based on a topic specified by the user of the application.
